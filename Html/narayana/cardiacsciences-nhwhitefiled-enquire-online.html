Key Cardiac Conditions & Procedures
Major Cardiac issues treated include,
  • Coronary Blockages
  • Valvular Regurgitation and leaking valves
  • Acute Coronary Syndrome
  • Acute myocardial infarction
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Palpitation
  • Flutter Arrhythmia
  • Heart Failures
  • Tight Lesions
Major Cardiac Procedures performed include,
  • Angiogram (CAG)
  • Angioplasty (PTCA)
  • Primary Angioplasty for acute heart attack
  • Electrophysiology Study, RF Ablation, ICD, CRT and 3D Mapping
  • Pacemaker Implant
  • Coronary Artery Bypass Graft (CABG)
  • Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) Surgery and Ventricle Septal Defect (VSD) Surgery
  • Vascular Surgeries, Mitral Valve Replacement (MVR)
  • Aortic Valve Replacement (AVR), Double Valve Repair (DVR)
  • Intra Cardiac Repairs
  • Aortic Aneurysm
